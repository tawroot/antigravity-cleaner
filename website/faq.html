Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common issues.

Is it safe to use?

Yes. Antigravity Cleaner specifically targets temporary files, caches, and invalid registry keys associated with known IDE errors. It does not touch your personal documents or project source codes.

Will I lose my browser passwords?

No. Our "Session Preservation" technology ensures that critical files like Cookies and Login Data are backed up or excluded from the cleaning process, so you don't have to log in again.

How do I fix "Network Error"?

Use the "Network Reset" feature in the Tools menu. This will flush your DNS cache and reset the Winsock catalog, which often resolves connection issues caused by VPNs using the Tun/Tap adapter.

Do you support Mac M1/M2?

Yes, we have native builds for Apple Silicon (ARM64) as well as Intel Macs. Check the Download page for the correct version.
